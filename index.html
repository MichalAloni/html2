Gymnastics

When I was 6, I asked my mom to sign me up for gymnastics class so I could be like my sister. From here to there, I trained with this group for 8 years. Training was intense - 3 times a week, 3 hours each. Persistence, hard work and precision in exercises were required to progress and improve.

Over the years, we have participated in many competitions across the country, and once we flew to London for training and meetings with other groups.
The decision to retire was not easy for me, and after a year I became a gymnastics guide for about 6 years. There will always be a special place in my heart for the gymnastics world.

Throughout the years, we performed across the country, and once we flew to Brazil to perform and meet with other groups.

Group Workouts

My first exposure to group training was 4 years ago. From then until today, I've worked out 3-4 times a week, in a variety of types of training: CrossFit, functional training, strength training, yoga, Pilates, etc. One of my favorite workout types is functional training, in which exercises are combined with different types of equipment.

Exercising makes me feel alive. I enjoy learning new exercises and working hard to get better and stronger. Gymnastics and dance have provided me with the strength and precision in movement needed for the training, as well as the persistence and the ability to work hard.
